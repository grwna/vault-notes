Perceptron is the simplest iteration of Artificial Neural Network

# Neuron
Formal definition (context: Binary Classification)
Linear combination of net input and bias:
$$z=w_{0}+w_{1}x_{1}+\dots+w_{m}x_{m}$$
Where x: real-valued input, w is weight and w0/b is threshold

The output uses a sign function based on the threshold

# Perceptron
- Simple neural network, ANN based on a unit.
- Represents hyperplane decision surface in n-dimensions (line, plane, etc.)
- Perceptron easily represent m-of-n function
Function where at least m of the n inputs must be true.
AND m=n 
OR m=1
